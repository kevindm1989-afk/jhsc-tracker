Privacy Policy — JHSC Advisor

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

1. Who Operates This App

JHSC Advisor is operated independently by the Worker Co-Chair of the Joint Health and Safety Committee (JHSC). This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any employer or union organization.

This app is maintained to support the JHSC's statutory obligations under the Occupational Health and Safety Act, R.S.O. 1990, c. O.1 (OHSA).

2. What Information We Collect

When you create an account:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)
  • Workplace department or shift (if provided)

When you use the app:

  • Hazard findings and safety reports you submit
  • Incident reports you submit
  • Worker statements you record
  • Suggestions you submit
  • Date and time of submissions

Anonymous submissions:

  • If you choose to submit anonymously, your name is not attached to the submission
  • Basic technical data (device type, browser) may still be collected for app functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for the following purposes:

  • To record and track hazard findings, incidents, and safety concerns on behalf of the JHSC
  • To allow the JHSC to follow up on action items and recommendations under OHSA s.9(21)
  • To maintain a record of worker statements related to workplace incidents
  • To support the JHSC's statutory functions under OHSA s.9
  • To communicate with you regarding your submissions if required

Your information is never used for:

  • Employer performance monitoring or discipline
  • Any purpose outside of JHSC health and safety functions
  • Sale or transfer to any third party

4. Who Can Access Your Information

  • Worker Co-Chair — full access for JHSC record-keeping purposes
  • JHSC Worker Members — access to relevant committee records
  • You — you can view your own submissions at any time

No employer or third party has access to this app, its data, or its records. This app operates on independently controlled infrastructure.

5. Anonymous Submissions

  • Your name will not be recorded or attached to the submission
  • The JHSC will act on the substance of your report without identifying you
  • Anonymous submissions cannot be followed up with you directly

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on privately controlled servers independent of any employer systems
  • Data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Passwords are stored using industry-standard encryption
  • Access to the database is restricted to the app operator only
  • Regular security practices are applied to protect your information

7. Data Retention

  • Hazard findings, incident reports, and worker statements are retained for the duration of the JHSC's operational needs and as required to fulfill statutory obligations under OHSA
  • If you request deletion of your personal information, it will be removed within 30 days unless retention is required by law
  • Anonymous submissions cannot be deleted as they contain no identifying information

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) and Ontario law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to the collection of your information at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact the Worker Co-Chair directly through your JHSC committee.

9. Cookies and Technical Data

This app may use minimal cookies or local storage to maintain your login session. No tracking cookies or advertising technologies are used. No data is shared with advertisers or analytics platforms.

10. Children

This app is intended for use by adult employees only. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 16.

11. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, notice will be posted within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact the Worker Co-Chair through your Joint Health and Safety Committee.

This privacy policy is maintained independently by the Worker Co-Chair of the Joint Health and Safety Committee.
