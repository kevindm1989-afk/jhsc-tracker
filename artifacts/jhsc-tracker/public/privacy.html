JHSC Advisor

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how JHSC Advisor (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application at https://jhscadvisor.com. JHSC Advisor is a workplace health and safety tracking tool designed to assist Joint Health & Safety Committees in meeting their obligations under the Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA) of Ontario, Canada.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the application:

  • Account information: your name, username, and email address used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Workplace safety records: inspection findings, hazard reports, corrective action items, and follow-up notes entered by authorized users.
  • Worker statements: right-to-refuse records and worker health and safety concern submissions.
  • Meeting data: JHSC meeting minutes, agenda items, motions, and attendance records.
  • Session data: login session tokens stored in a secure, server-side session store.
  • Technical data: basic server access logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) retained for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating users and maintaining secure access to the application.
  • Storing, organizing, and displaying workplace health and safety records.
  • Enabling JHSC members to track open and closed action items, hazard findings, and inspection logs.
  • Supporting compliance with Ontario’s Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA S.9 / O. Reg. 297/13).
  • Sending transactional emails (e.g., password resets) through a secure email service provider.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to occupational health and safety compliance.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on encrypted servers hosted in a secure cloud environment.
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text; they are hashed using bcrypt before storage.
  • User sessions are managed using server-side session tokens with secure, HTTP-only cookies.
  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS (HTTPS).
  • Access to the application is restricted to authorized personnel with valid credentials.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Your data may be accessed by:

  • Authorized application administrators within your organization for the purpose of managing user accounts and resolving technical issues.
  • Infrastructure and email service providers (such as cloud hosting and transactional email services) who process data strictly on our behalf and are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities if required by applicable Ontario or Canadian law.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information in your account.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting your organization’s JHSC administrator or emailing us at [your-email@domain.com].

Workplace safety records (inspection logs, hazard findings, meeting minutes, etc.) may be retained by your organization as required for OHSA compliance, even after an individual account is removed.

6. Cookies & Session Data

JHSC Advisor uses a single session cookie to maintain your login state. This cookie is:

  • HTTP-only (not accessible to JavaScript)
  • Transmitted only over HTTPS
  • Automatically expired after a period of inactivity (15 minutes) or when you sign out

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party tracking cookies.

7. Children’s Privacy

JHSC Advisor is intended for use by adult workplace health and safety committee members and employees. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
